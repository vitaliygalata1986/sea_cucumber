Vision and Mission

Since 2020, we have been harvesting high-quality sea
cucumbers from Sri Lanka, ensuring Swiss standards
of food processing, diligence, and technology, enhanced
by the rich cultural heritage of Sri Lanka

Our efforts have elevated the livelihoods of local harvesting
communities in Kalpitiya, Jaffna, and Trincomalee. Additionally,
we support education for the children of these communities
by providing school materials for all year long for over 5 years
of schooling term.

A marine delicacy with a rich culinary history,
has been a staple in many Asian cuisines.

Often found in soups and stews, it is celebrated for
its unique texture and remarkable ability
to absorb flavors.

Beyond its culinary allure, the Sea Cucumber offers numerous
health benefits.

Traditionally used in Chinese medicine, it is valued for its high
collagen content, which supports skin health, boosts the
immune system, and promotes joint function due to its anti
-inflammatory properties.

Acquaculture 2026

in 2026, we aim to establish a state-of-the-art aquaculture and processing facility for
sea cucumbers in Mohawatturam, Kalpitiya Region, spanning 25 acres.
This project aims to ensure top-notch quality and year-round maintenance, further
elevating our commitment to sustainable practices and premium product standards.
By focusing on sustainable aquaculture, we aim to reduce the pressure on wild sea
cucumber populations and promote biodiversity.

This facility will incorporate eco-friendly technologies and practices, ensuring minimal
environmental impact and contributing to the long-term health of marine ecosystems.
